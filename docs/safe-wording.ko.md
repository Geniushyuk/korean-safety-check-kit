# Safe wording rules

Korean Safety Check Kit is for pause-and-check UX, not diagnosis or legal judgment.

## Do

- Use calm, non-judgmental Korean.
- Tell users they can stop responding, stop sending money, and verify through official channels.
- Prefer action verbs: 멈추기, 기록하기, 공유하기, 공식번호로 확인하기.
- Mention official emergency resources when risk is high.
- Preserve user agency: “응하지 않아도 됩니다”, “통화를 끊어도 됩니다”.

## Do not

- Do not promise that a situation is definitely safe or definitely criminal.
- Do not blame the user for clicking, sending, meeting, or trusting.
- Do not tell users to confront an attacker alone.
- Do not replace police, medical, counseling, legal, or emergency services.
- Do not include unverified phone numbers or private contact information.

## Preferred Korean tone

- Good: “지금은 송금, 추가 자료 전송, 이동을 멈추고 기록을 남기세요.”
- Bad: “당신은 사기를 당했습니다.”
- Good: “긴급 위험이 있으면 112/119 등 공식 도움을 먼저 이용하세요.”
- Bad: “이 체크만 따르면 안전합니다.”
